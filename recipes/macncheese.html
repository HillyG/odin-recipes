MACNCHEESE

Description

A mac and cheese recipe that everybody will love

simple,cheap and tasty as fuck

Ingredients

  • 1 package of mac
  • 1 package of cheese
  • 1 bottle of milk
  • 1 package of butter
  • 4 tbspn of flour
  • spices
    • rosemary
    • black pepper
    • turmeric
    • red pepper
  • chicken breast 200 gr optional

Preparation

  1. grate all the cheese
  2. boil your chicken breasts for 5 minutes if you have any
  3. slice your chicken in very small pieces
  4. get a pan pour some oil in it and start to heat it up on the low temperature
  5. after 5 minutes of heating up, add all the listed spices and pinch of salt as well
  6. cook those spices till you see bubbles everywhere
  7. add chicken breasts in it mix it up and leave it on low temp
  8. start to cook your pasta
  9. get the sauce
